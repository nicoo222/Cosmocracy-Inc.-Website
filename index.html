About

Cosmocracy Inc. is a French music band from Nancy composed of 4 musicians. Founded in 2016, the band crosses various musical horizons. Although we are all being influenced by great Rock bands from 60s and 70s (The Doors, Pink Floyd, The Who, etc.), every member brings his own background and thus contributes to create new musical atmospheres. It is difficult to characterize our music, but it can be related to “Progressive Rock” and “Psychedelic Rock” including some “Funk”, “Stoner” and “Post-Rock” elements.

Our first album “A Ride Across Your Mind” released on 25 January 2019. Everything was made by ourselves (composition, recording, mix, mastering, and design). Such a work has been a great opportunity to get a better vision and control of our sound. We wanted to make an album in which each track has a strong identity. We chose to publish it under a Creative Commons Licence (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) to let people use or adapt our work, in a desire of artistic share and collaboration.

We love to include jam sessions during our playing. It is a great way to be spontaneous and listen to each other. Some improvised sections are part of our compositions. This allows us to deliver a different version every time we play a title.
